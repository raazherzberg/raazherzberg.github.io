Remindly was developed in a 48 hours hackathon with 3 more team members.

Remindly is a MyMe based mobile app that enriches your social encounters.
MyMe is a tiny AI camera, capable of connecting with your phone and recognising
faces at real time. You can read more on their website.

Remindly help you enrich your social encounters by providing tools for social based alerts.
Remindly allows you to define rules and reminders on the people you meet.
Once you meet somebody you have set a rule/alert on, Remindly will use a smartwatch
to notify you in a quite and un-intrusive ways.

Remindly opens a wide range of new possibilities to enhance our real-world social experience
