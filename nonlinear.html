Non-Linear Story
Description: An interactive board and cubes game.
Generating a story based on Peter and the Wolf.
Technical information: Arduino, Processing, Adobe Flash, ActionScript
Game Cubes
Decoration cubes
Decision cubes
Technical information
Board & flow information
Placing a cube closes either one or two circuits, depending on the cube's orientation.

The number of closed circuits informs us of the player's choice.
The next part of the story is determined using the cube's orientation and then revealed.

The story is built by a decision tree.
