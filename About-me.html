Hello!

I was born in Poland, but my educational path has lead me to live in Italy and the US. I'm currently living and working in London.

I love to learn, especially about how the world and the things in it work, and how we, humans, communicate. I speak 4 languages fluently: Polish, English, German and Italian. I can communicate in basic French and Russian, and I started to pick up some Spanish and Chinese (fun fact: Panda in Chinese is composed of two characters: "bear" + "cat"! 熊猫). I also love learning about our planet and broadly understood geography (check out my What on Earth? project!). I read a lot, and buy many more books than I read.

I'm always up for a challenge, be it creative, physical or intellectual.

In design I strive to create work that will be useful, entertaining, beautiful, and insightful. I want to expand the user's knowledge, spark curiosity and leave a lasting, positive attitude towards the subject treated.

Let's go exploring!
